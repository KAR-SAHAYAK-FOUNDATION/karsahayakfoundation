Who We Are

KAR SAHAYAK FOUNDATION is a public charitable trust committed to creating an informed, financially aware and empowered society.

We work at the intersection of financial literacy, taxation awareness, education, digital inclusion and community welfare.

Our initiatives are designed to simplify complex financial and legal concepts, improve access to education and government welfare schemes, support vulnerable communities and promote sustainable social development.

Through awareness programs, training sessions, educational resources, community outreach and welfare initiatives, we strive to bridge knowledge gaps and create opportunities for individuals from all sections of society, particularly students, women, senior citizens, small taxpayers, workers and economically disadvantaged communities.

Vision

To build an inclusive and empowered society where every individual possesses the knowledge, confidence and opportunities necessary to achieve financial security, educational growth and social well-being.

Mission

  • Promote financial literacy and responsible financial practices.
  • Simplify taxation and compliance awareness for the general public.
  • Improve access to education, learning resources and skill development.
  • Support digital literacy and financial inclusion.
  • Assist economically weaker sections in accessing government schemes and services.
  • Foster community welfare through health, livelihood and social support initiatives.
  • Encourage ethical values, responsible citizenship and sustainable development.

What We Do

Practical programs that make financial knowledge, education, digital tools and welfare support easier to access.

Financial Literacy & Tax Awareness

We conduct workshops, awareness campaigns and educational programs on:

  • Income Tax
  • GST
  • TDS
  • Advance Tax
  • Personal Finance
  • Budgeting
  • Savings & Investments
  • Financial Planning

Helping citizens understand their financial responsibilities and rights.

Education & Learning Support

We support students and learners through:

  • Scholarships
  • Educational Assistance
  • Study Materials
  • Digital Learning Resources
  • Career Guidance
  • Commerce and Finance Education

Our objective is to ensure that financial constraints do not become barriers to learning.

Digital Inclusion

We help communities adapt to the digital economy through training on:

  • Digital Payments
  • Online Banking
  • E-Governance Services
  • Government Portals
  • E-Filing Systems
  • Online Safety and Awareness

Particularly focusing on rural communities, women and senior citizens.

Skill Development & Livelihood Support

We work towards enhancing employability and entrepreneurship through:

  • Vocational Training
  • Entrepreneurship Awareness
  • Professional Development Programs
  • Career Skills Workshops
  • Livelihood Promotion Initiatives

Community Welfare

Our welfare initiatives may include:

  • Food Distribution
  • Health Camps
  • Medical Assistance
  • Disaster Relief
  • Women's Empowerment Programs
  • Senior Citizen Support
  • Child Welfare Initiatives

Public Awareness

We simplify important financial, legal, educational and welfare information so people can make better decisions with confidence.
